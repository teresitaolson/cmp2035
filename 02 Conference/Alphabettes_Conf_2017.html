About the Alphabettes Conference

The Alphabettes Design Conference is a 3-day design festival devoted to contemporary typography with talks, and tours focusing on where typography is today and where its future may lie. It will be held at Massachusetts College of Art in Boston.

Getting Here

Massachusetts College of Art and Design is located at 621 Huntington Ave, Boston, MA 02115.

FROM MASS TURNPIKE EAST, ROUTE 90 Take exit 22, Prudential Center/Copley Square. Stay to the left (Prudential Center).Follow ramp to Huntington Avenue. Continue straight on Huntington Avenue to MassArt on right, two blocks after the Museum of Fine Arts.

FROM THE SOUTH, ROUTES 3, 24, 93 Follow route 93 north (Southeast Expressway) toward Boston.Take exit 18, Mass Ave. Follow Frontage Road to third set of lights. Take left, to Mass Ave. Follow ramp to first set of lights, crossing Massachusetts Avenue to Melnea Cass Boulevard. Follow Melnea Cass Boulevard to Tremont Street. Turn left on Tremont Street and drive one block. Turn right onto Ruggles Street.Follow Ruggles Street to Huntington Avenue (at trolley tracks).Take a left onto Huntington Avenue. MassArt is one block up on the right.

FROM THE NORTH, ROUTES 95/128, 93 Upon approaching the city limits take Leverett Circle exit. Follow signs to Storrow Drive. Travel Storrow Drive to Copley Square/Back Bay exit. Turn Right onto Beacon Street.Follow Beacon Street to Exeter Street. Take a left onto Exeter Street and follow to Huntington Avenue. Take a right onto Huntington Avenue and follow it to the college. MassArt is on the right, 2 blocks after the Museum of Fine Arts.

PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION Take MBTA Green "E" line to the Longwood Medical Area stop or take the #39 or CT2 bus. For information and fares, visit the MBTA website at www.mbta.com
